Hi there! I'm Hillel Steinmetz (but, I prefer to go by Hilly), and I'm University of Chicago graduate with one year of research experience and a deep curiosity about the world.

I have a good amount of experience with writing and editing. I especially love creating stories out of technical information. I wrote and edited for the Chicago Maroon, my school newspaper, from 2015 to 2017. You can check out my articles in the newspaper here.

I also have experience with data manipulation, data visualization, and statistical analysis. I can work with R, SPSS, Excel, and I have some programming background as well with Python, Javascript, and C (which came in handy while making this website).

Most importantly, I love to challenge myself, and I'm always welcome opportunities to learning new things.

If you want to see some evidence of my progamming skills, take a look at a quiz game [ repository ] I am in the process of working on.

The quiz has data from 655 languages! (Although about 450 are pretty obscure...) The harder it gets, the more points you score. Good luck!
