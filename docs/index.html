Hi there! I'm Hilly, and I'm a technical writer and graduate student at the University of Washington. For my current position, I write and edit software documentation. At UW, I'm working towards a masters in computational linguistics.

I made a silly quiz game that you should feel free to play! Click here or the **Language Quiz** button in the navigation bar.

The quiz game tests your knowledge of the world's languages. You have to guess the country a language is spoken in, what family it belongs to, or identify the language from the script it commonly uses.

The quiz has data from 655 languages (although 450 of them are pretty obscure). The harder the quiz gets, the more points you score. Good luck!

You're welcome to look at the GitHub repository of the quiz (and website).
